Frequently Asked Questions

When does my plan change take effect?

Your plan change takes effect immediately. You'll have access to new features right away.

How does proration work?

If you upgrade mid-cycle, we calculate the difference between plans and apply it to your next billing date. If you downgrade, you receive a credit.

Can I change my plan back?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Each change is calculated with proration.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data remains safe. However, some features may be restricted based on your new plan. Contact support if you need help.
